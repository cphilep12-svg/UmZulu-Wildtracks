What to Bring

Pack light, pack smart. Here's everything you need for an unforgettable safari experience.

Clothing

  • Neutral colors (khaki, olive)
  • Light layers
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Wide-brimmed hat
  • Sunglasses

Equipment

  • Camera with zoom lens
  • Binoculars (we provide too)
  • Sunscreen (SPF 30+)
  • Insect repellent
  • Personal medications
