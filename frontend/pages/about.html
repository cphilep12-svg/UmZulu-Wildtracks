About UmZulu

Our Journey

UmZulu Wildtrack was founded by Mr Isiphile Simelane with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a safari experience that not only showcases the incredible wildlife of South Africa but actively contributes to its protection.

Located in the heart of KwaZulu-Natal, our private resort spans over 12,400 hectares of pristine wilderness. In just a few short years, we've grown from a dream into a thriving conservation-focused tourism destination.

Every guest who visits UmZulu becomes part of our conservation story. Your safari experience directly funds anti-poaching patrols, wildlife monitoring, and community education programs.

Mr Isiphile Simelane

Founder & Managing Director

Conservation

Protecting Our Wildlife

Conservation is at the heart of everything we do at UmZulu Wildtrack. We believe that responsible tourism can be a powerful force for protecting endangered species and preserving natural habitats.

  • 24/7 anti-poaching patrols
  • Wildlife monitoring and research
  • Habitat restoration projects
  • Community education programs
  • Endangered species protection

Your visit directly supports these initiatives, making you a vital partner in our conservation efforts.

Community

Local Impact

We believe that conservation succeeds when local communities thrive. That's why we're committed to creating employment opportunities and supporting the communities around our reserve.

  • 90% of our staff are from local communities
  • Skills development and training programs
  • Support for local schools
  • Sustainable livelihood initiatives
